About

In the ongoing scenario, as we can see that people are getting dependent on AI for basic needs like shopping, booking, travelling etc. this application aims to provide growth in the fitness industry by thus reaching out to people.

We present “AI Trainer”, an application based on the MediaPipe Holistic pipeline for quantifying fitness tracking that is developed for real-time estimation. For an individual, 33 key body joints are generated by the AI Trainer during estimation and gives about 15-20 frames per second on CPU. Therefore, it is suited for both recorded and live videos of fitness exercises such as dumbbell curls, squats etc . Our prime endowments include a methodology for accurate fitness tracking solution that uses both regression to key joint coordinates and heatmaps.
